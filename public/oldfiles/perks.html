What is Perks?

Perks automates the management of your Twitter account. We find valuable followers and follow them in return, curate your friends, and direct message potential clients. You focus on content and Perks handles the rest.

Who is it for?

Love twitter and all of its 'perks', but find it cumbersome to search out valuable followers? Perks is perfect for those who use Twitter as a marketing tool for their business.

Why use it?

Twitter can be an excellent marketing tool for any business. Optimizing your twitter account on your own is time consuming though, and we understand that your time is valuable. Let Perks handle the messy part so you can focus on your business.
